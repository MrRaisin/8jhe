Athens

Athens is the Capital City of Greece. The Ancient Greeks named the city after Athena,the Greek godess of battle strategy and wisdom. Athens was home to some great philsophers including Aristotle and Plato. Athens has some beautiful architecture such as the Parthenonon Acropolis hill,the Temple of Olympian Zeus and the Theatre of Dionysos at the bottom of Acropolis dedicated to the Greek God Dionysos.
