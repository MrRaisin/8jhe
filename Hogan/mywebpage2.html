Football

Fact 1: Football originated in China around 476 B.C.

Fact 2: Football is the most played and most watched sport on earth.

Fact 3: Football is the most popular sport in the world. Over one billion fans watch World Cup Football on television.

Fact 4: The largest Football tournament saw no less than 5,098 teams. They competed in 1999 for the second Bangkok League Seven-a-Side Competition. Over 35,000 players participated.

Fact 5: The maximum number of goals scored by one player in a single Football match was 16. It was scored by Stephan Stanis (France) playing for Racing Club de Lens in December 1942

Fact 6: Based on video evidence, one of the fastest ever scored was in 2.8 seconds by Ricardo Olivera (Uruguay) in December 1998.

Fact 7: Football goalies didn't have to wear different coloured shirts from their teammates until 1913.

Fact 8: Football players run an average of 9.65 kms during every game.

Fact 9: The very first game of basketball was played with a soccer ball.

Fact 10: The World's First Football Club was the English Sheffield Football Club. It was founded in 1857 by Colonel Nathaniel Cresswick and Major William Priest, two British Army officers.
