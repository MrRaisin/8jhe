Basketball is a game played in hundreds of countries around the world

  • It is most popular in the USA, The Philippines, Puerto Rico, South Korea and Spain.
  • The first hoops were actually just peach baskets and the first backboards were made of wire.
  • The game became an official Olympic event at the Summer Games in Germany in 1936.
  • The NBA instituted the three-pointer before the 1979-80 season.
  • Michael Jordan has scored more points in the playoffs than any other player.
