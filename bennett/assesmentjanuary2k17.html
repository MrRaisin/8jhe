London

London is the capital city of England and the most populated city in England and the United Kingdom. The River Thames runs through London, starting in a meadow in the Costwolds Hills in Gloucestershire travelling 215 miles, before emptying in the North Sea near Essex. London has the population of estimated around 8.5 million. Some of the top tourist attractions in Lodnon include, Big Ben, London Eye, Madame Tussauds, Buckingham Palace and much more! London was founded by the Romans originally being called Londinium.
