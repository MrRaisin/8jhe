Paris

Paris is the is the capital city of France and the most populated city of the country. It has a population of around 2.5 million and is a beautiful area to visit. Famous landmarks in Paris include; The Eiffel Tower and the 12th century, Gothic Notre-Dame Catherdrial, the Louvre Museum, the Paris catocombes and the magical DisneyLand. In 2014 Paris was the most popular tourist destination in the world, with an estimated 32.2 million tourists!
